How It Works

CucumberMe, the service that sends cucumbers anonymously to a friend, enemy, or ex. Send a cucumber with some personality.

Step 1 – Choose Your Cucumber

Click the button below. Select through 4 exciting size options that best suit your cucumber needs.

Step 2 – Write a clever message

After selecting your cucumber you will be prompted to enter a message that will be written on your cucumber. So a simple – "Netflix and chill?" can really get your message across.

Step 3 – Address of who you want to prank

During checkout you will be prompted to input the address of the person to whom you will send the cucumber.

Faq

  • How will the cucumber be delivered?

    Your cucumber will be mailed anonymously in a box that fits the size you chose above.

  • When will my cucumber be sent out after I order?

    Your cucumber will be sent A.S.A.P.

  • What if the cucumber spoils during shipping?

    If it does, it will be a bigger insult to the person receiving it.

  • How can you track shipment?

    You will receive an e-mail with the tracking information of your cucumber.

  • How is my message going to be printed?

    Your message will be written on the cucumber via metallic silver acrylic marker.

  • Do we accept returns?

    Unforunately we do not accept returns. Sorry for the inconvenience.

  • Can you eat the cucumbers?

    Please refrain from eating the cucumbers. They will be written on and may absorb the chemicals used in markers.
